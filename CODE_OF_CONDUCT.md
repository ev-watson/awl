# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, technical, and good-faith collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Focus on the code, the problem, and the evidence.
- Assume mistakes before malice.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation
- Bad-faith trolling or deliberate derailment
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeatedly ignoring repository standards after moderation

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior harms the project or other contributors.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `etwatson@ucsd.edu`.
